Redefining the interactive classroom

A tool for teachers and students to facilitate learning in real time





LEARNING IN REAL TIME

For Teachers


Monitor your students' level of understanding while you teach. The platform gives you instant feedback, allowing you to adjust your class speed and depth dynamically.

  • HUD of all the students in your class.
  • Understanding graded by color for analysis at a glance.
  • Analytics and tracking - record your sessions for later review.

For Students


Students can get the help they need, when they need it. Peer interaction facilitates learning and promotes collaboration.

  • Simpe, intuitive interface that won't distract from your class.
  • Peer-to-peer, 1-on-1 chat gets students unstuck faster and reduces class interruptions.
  • Monitor which students need help and which ones are helping their peers.
